(Elevator)

-Move toward elevator call buttons
	-Press top button to go up
	-Press bottom button to go down
-Wait for elevator
-When doors open, step inside 
	-Press the button that corresponds to desired floor
-When doors open, step outside



(Turn car on/Start driving)

-Walk up to car
-Insert key in door
	-Turn key right to unlock
	-Take key out of door
-Open door
-Get inside of car 
-Insert key in ignition
	-Turn key to the right and hold
	-Release after three seconds
-Press and hold the brake pedal
	-Move stick shift to "D"
	-Release brake pedal
-Press gas pedal 
	-Steer
